Activities I like

It hurts, but I keep doing it

I like to swim, cycle and run. I am in training for my first triathlon, but it is hard work.

Kinds of Triathlon There are different kinds of triathon - sprint, Olympic and so on. I am aiming for Olympic, which consists of the following:
  1. 1.5 km swim
  2. 4o0 km cycle
  3. 10 km run
